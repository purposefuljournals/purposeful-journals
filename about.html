The Author

A note from
Ceblle Dawson

I made this for you.

Not for the version of you that has it all together. For the one you are right now — navigating the pressure, sitting with the chaos, carrying the wounds, and still showing up anyway.

Every book in this store was born from a real tension that I know personally. And every one was written with the same belief at its center — that you are more than what you are carrying. That you are worthy of the time it takes to sit with yourself. That the most important work you will ever do is the work of understanding who you are becoming.

These pages are your space. Use them honestly. Show up for yourself the way you show up for everyone else.

It is your turn.
